
core concept
Physics based archery game. Loosely inspired by rangers apprentice.
Players need to shoot one or several arrows to achieve a goal/solve a puzzle

level ideas
short range and long range shots
hitting through a small gap
shooting through moving platforms
shooting on a moving platform

limitations
shooting an animal will result in a lose
running out of arrows results in a lose

art style
minimalistic art style with dithered shadows
